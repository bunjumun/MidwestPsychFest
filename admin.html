Create a payment link in your Square Dashboard ↗ and paste it here. The bar page will append the itemized order as a note.

How It Works

  1. Customer visits bar.html (linked from the 🍺 FAB)
  2. Age gate confirms 21+ via sessionStorage
  3. Customer builds order from data/menu.json
  4. Checkout constructs itemized note + total and redirects to your Square link
  5. Square collects payment; bartender fulfills order
