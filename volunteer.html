Google Sheet Configuration

Paste the published CSV URL from your Google Sheet (see instructions below). This URL is stored locally in your browser and is never committed to the repo.

How to get your Google Sheet CSV URL

  1. Open your volunteer Google Sheet.
  2. Go to File → Share → Publish to web.
  3. Under "Link", choose the sheet tab, then select Comma-separated values (.csv).
  4. Click Publish and copy the URL.
  5. Paste it above and click Save & Test.

Alternatively, use the export URL format:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/export?format=csv&gid=0

Leave as defaults (A–F) if your sheet follows the recommended layout.
